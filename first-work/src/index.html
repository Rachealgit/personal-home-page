Educational History:

Attended Orhue Primary School where she gained her first school leaving certificate (1995), also attended Baptist High School and obtained her National Examination Certificate (2001), proceeded to University of Portharcourt and acquired a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science (2009). Then in 2019, she obtained her Masters in Information Technology (MSc Info. Tech) from Open University of Nigeria.

Job Experience:

Racheal’s interest in Computer Technology was from childhood curiousity, fascinated about computer games and video games at its early stage. Her keen interest was to learn about computer and its diversity to bring a change to the world using technological tools. Her passion for IT got her a job with the Edo State Government Information Technology Agency, and was later absorb into the Edo Civil Service Commission in the Ministry of Budget and Economic Planning as a Program Analyst, which afforded her the opportunity to showcase her skill in the Nigeria Erosion Watershed Management Project (NEWMAP) as the ICT/MIS officer and also was recommended to head the MIS Unit of a Federal Government Program called Household Uplifting Program in Nigeria, Edo State.
